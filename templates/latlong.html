Find Your Camera's Coordinates
Coming soon: This page will let you search by address or place name and click a map to get precise coordinates. For now, use one of the methods below.
Option 1 — Google Maps
  1. Open Google Maps.
  2. Navigate to your camera's location.
  3. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) directly on the spot.
  4. The coordinates appear at the top of the menu — click them to copy.
Option 2 — Your IP Camera's Admin Page

Many IP cameras (Reolink, Foscam, Hikvision) show GPS coordinates in their web interface under Settings → Location or Device Info. Check your camera's admin page first — the coordinates may already be there.
